How It Works

MemeMinter.fun makes it easy to create your own meme coin in just a few simple steps!

Step 1: Choose Your Token Name & Symbol

Pick a unique name and symbol for your meme coin.

Step 2: Select a Blockchain Network

Choose the blockchain where your token will be created. We currently support:

  • Solana
  • Ethereum
  • Polygon

Step 3: Set Token Parameters

Define details like total supply, decimals, and any special features.

Step 4: Click "Mint"

Once you're ready, click the "Mint Your Meme Coin" button, and your token will be generated instantly!

Step 5: Trade & Grow Your Coin

After minting, you can list your token on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) and build your community.
